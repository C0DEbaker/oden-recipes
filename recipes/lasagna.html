Recipe for Lasagna

  1. Make sausage
  2. Brown sausage, then add garlic and shallots
  3. Add can of San Marzano tomatoes + can of tomato paste + 1/2 can of tomato sauce
  4. Season Appropriately
  5. After letting your sauce cook down a bit, boil the noodles and let them rest in a pot of cold water
  6. Assemble lasagna with gratuitous amounts of mozzarella, ricotta, and parmesean
  7. Bake at 375F for 45 minutes covered with foil
  8. Broil the top with extra cheese until it looks like a tasty slice of cheese pizza.
  9. Wait for it to cool a bit before you try eating it!
