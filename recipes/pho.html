The Best Goddamn Pho You Ever Ate

Recipe

  • Wash whole chicken
  • Boil chicken in water
  • Roast small piece of peeled ginger and one onion over stove fire until charred
  • Put ginger and onion into water with chicken
  • Skim off blood foam as the chicken boils, making sure to occasionally flip chicken
  • Remove chicken from soup once cooked
  • In a small cast-iron, heat spices to release aromas then add to broth
  • Cook noodles to manufacturer instructions
  • Serve hot with fresh herbs and chilis!
