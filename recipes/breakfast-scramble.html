Breakfast Scramble

"This scramble will take your morning to the next level"-local yolked man

The actual recipe

  • Crack two eggs in small bowl, lightly beat to combine then set aside
  • Dice up a quarter onion, a handful of mushrooms, and a slice of ham
  • Rough chop a handful of arugula
  • Now saute your onions and mushrooms together in butter over medium-low until soft and fragrant
  • Add your ham, then the arugula and cook until the arugula turns a vibrant green,
  • Now add your egg mixture and delicately fold them into your sauted mixture
  • Cook just until fluffy and soft making sure not to brown the eggs.
  • Enjoy with salsa and cheese topping of choice!
